''Summoned near water

Base Stats
----------

-   Body Size:Large
-   Weight:300
-   Defense:15
-   Damage:40
-   Ranged Resistance:Yes
-   Skill:Swimming

Summary
-------

You can summon these near water using a summon elemental spell. They are
dangerous and have a considerable damage stat. They cross water with
ease, due to their liquid form.
