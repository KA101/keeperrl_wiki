Base Stats
----------

-   Defense:10
-   Damage:10
-   Body size:Large
-   Weight:400
-   Fast:True
-   Animal:Yes
-   Chasing:No

Summary
-------

Fun to shoot down with a bow, if you can find them. They are a lot
easier to spot from the air. Not a danger unless you blunder into one
without any armour on. Imps don't like them much.
