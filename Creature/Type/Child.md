Summary
-------

Peasant children are stealthy and make good food for dopplegangers.

Base Stats
----------

-   Damage:8
-   Body size:Medium
-   Defense:8
-   Skill:Stealth

Child (spider food)
===================

-   Damage:2
-   Body size:Medium
-   Entangled:Yes
-   Blind:Yes
-   Defense:2

Don't absorb the spider food unless you want a blind doppleganger.
