A succubus is a large humanoid spirit with 2 arms, 2 legs and 2 wings. It is agile and fast, but weak.

A succubus can give birth to other creatures including:
* [[Harpy]]
* [[Ogre]]
* [[Orc Shaman]]
* [[Werewolf]]
