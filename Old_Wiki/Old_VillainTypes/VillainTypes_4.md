=Usage=
Used to specify what category a villain will appear as on a campaign map.

=Example from campaign_villains.txt=
 DUKE4 KNIGHTS &quot;Knights&quot; '''MAIN'''

=Valid values=
*MAIN
*LESSER
*ALLY
