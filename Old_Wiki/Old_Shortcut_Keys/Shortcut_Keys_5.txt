=Keeper Mode Keys=

==Build Menu==
*a - Prioritise task
*b - Bed
*c - Torch
*d - Toggle Dig/Cut
*e - Remove Construction
*f - Floors
*g - Graveyard
*i - Summon imp
*l - Research
*m - Manufacturies
*s - storage
*t - training
*u - torture
*l - library
*Shift - Select rectangle (with mouse)

==Scrolling==
*z - zoom
*Arrow Left - Scroll Left
*Arrow Right - Scroll Right
*Arrow Up - Scroll Up
*Arrow Down - Scroll Down
*Numpad - Scroll around

==Game speed==
1 - Slow
2 - Normal
3 - Fast
4 - Very Fast
*Space - Pause

==Game Menu==
Escape - Game menu
