=Usage=
Used to specify what selection of allies and villains appear for a particular type of keeper or adventurer.

=Example from player_creatures.txt=
 creatureId = { KEEPER_MAGE KEEPER_MAGE_F }
 tribeAlignment = '''EVIL'''

=Valid values=
*EVIL
*LAWFUL
