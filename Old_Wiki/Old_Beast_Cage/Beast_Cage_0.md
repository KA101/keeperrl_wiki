Beast lair is the dormitory of:

* [[Raven|Ravens]]
* [[Wolf|Wolfs]]
* [[Cave Bear|Cave Bears]]
* [[Legendary Beast|Legendary Beasts]]

== Attraction ==
All beasts except werewolves only need a free space in the dorm for their cages to [[Immigration|immigrate]].
