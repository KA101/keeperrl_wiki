Description: ''Beasts have their cages here.''


Beast lair is the dormitory of:
* [[Bat|Bats]]
* [[Raven|Ravens]]
* [[Wolf|Wolfs]]
* [[Cave Bear|Cave Bears]]
* [[Werewolf|Werewolfs]]
* [[Legendary Beast|Legendary Beasts]]

== Attraction ==
All beasts except werewolfs only need a free space in the dorm for their cages to [[Immigration|immigrante]].

== Tile Cost ==
12 Wood planks
