=Usage=
Used to specify what traits immigrants have

=Example from immigration.txt=
 traits = { FIGHTER NO_AUTO_EQUIPMENT }

=Valid Values=
*LEADER
*FIGHTER
*WORKER
*PRISONER
*NO_EQUIPMENT
*NO_LIMIT
*FARM_ANIMAL
*SUMMONED
*NO_AUTO_EQUIPMENT
*DOESNT_TRIGGER
*INCREASE_POPULATION
