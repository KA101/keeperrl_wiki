Description: ''Mana is regenerated here.''

Allows your keeper to research new [[Technologies|technologies]].

The maximum amount of technologies the library can store is:
 ([library tiles in dungeon] + 1) / 2

Each creature studying in the library generates mana.
 

==Tile Cost==
20 wood

==Efficiency==
Yes
