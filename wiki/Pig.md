Base Stats
==========

-   Defense:2
-   Damage:5
-   Body size:Medium
-   Weight:150
-   Minion Food:Yes
-   Slow:Yes
-   Chasing:No
-   Animal:Yes

Summary
-------

These creatures can be conjured in a pigsty and provide a free source of
nutrition for your minions, leaving them a little happier, stronger and
able to focus on killing other tribes.
