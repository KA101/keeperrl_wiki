- Weight: 0.1
- Defence : 0
- Price: 10 gold
- Type: Ring
- Special Powers: Makes you fearless
- Concept: A very cheap ring that prevents some monsters from getting scared.
- Purpose in game: Low cost, small benefit, decent starter rings
