Usage
=====

Used to specify what can be built from the build menu and also for
immigration requirements.

Example from build\_menu.txt
============================

`\{ Furniture \{\{ `**`MOUNTAIN`**` \} STONE 5 \} `“`Soft`` ``rock`”` `“`Structure`”` \}`
