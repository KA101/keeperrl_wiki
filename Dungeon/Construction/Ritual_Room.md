placing demon shrines:

-   [Ghost](/keeperrl_wiki/Ghost "wikilink")

-   [Doppleganger](/keeperrl_wiki/Doppleganger "wikilink")

<!-- -->

-   30 gold per shrine
-   Requirement: Demonology
