The Orders menu contains the following tabs:

-   Claim Tile  
-   Fetch Items  
-   Fetch Items Persistently  
-   Prioritize Task  
-   Remove Construction  
-   Forbid Zone  

pressing the “a” key brings up the menu with Prioritize Task selected
