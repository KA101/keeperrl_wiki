Usage
=====

Used with [ItemTypes](/keeperrl_wiki/ItemTypes "wikilink") to specify variations of
items

Example from workshops\_menus.txt
=================================

`\{\{Potion `**`Heal`**`\} 4 GOLD 10 `“`advanced`` ``alchemy`”`\}`

Valid values
============

-   Teleport
-   Heal
-   Fire
-   DestroyEquipment
-   EnhanceArmor
-   EnhanceWeapon
-   Suicide
-   EmitPoisonGas
-   CircularBlast
-   Deception
-   Summon
-   SummonElement
-   Acid
-   Alarm
-   TeleEnemies
-   SilverDamage
-   CurePoison
-   Lasting
-   Permanent
-   PlaceFurniture
-   Damage
-   InjureBodyPart
-   LooseBodyPart
-   RegrowBodyPart
-   DestroyWalls
