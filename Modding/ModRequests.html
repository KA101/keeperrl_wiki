  • 🏰 Abandoned Dungeons: Adds abandoned dungeon pieces to z-levels for new designs.
  • 📈 More Z-levels: Adds additional z-levels, no new items or units.
  • 🎮 CreativeModeMod: Adds creative mode, no additional features.
  • 🕷️ Alpha30SpiderMod: Play as Spider Queen, making her a proper keeper.
  • 🐾 Immigration Creature Sprites: Adds variety of sprites for immigration creatures.
  • 🦀 Bandits to Mr. Crabs: Bandits’ sprites replaced with Mr. Crabs for swarming behavior.
  • ☁️ Heaven and Hell Mod: Check general details from related messages.
  • 🐉 Totem Summons: Summon loyal beasts or magical creatures using a totem.
  • 🩸 Blood Ritual Mod: Use bloody water symbols for dungeon-wide buffs.
  • 💀 Auto Raise Skeletons Mod: Raises skeletons from graveyards as low HP fodder.
  • ⚔️ Hostile Unit Spawners: Spawners create hostile units for defense or entertainment.
  • 🐲 Kobold Keeper Mod: Kobold Keeper inspired by D&D or Pathfinder Kobolds.
  • 🚧 Guard Zone Selector: Choose specific minions for guard zones strategically.
  • 🍄 Post-win Potion Crafting: Add late-game potion crafting using mushrooms and custom resources.
